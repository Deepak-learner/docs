# Team's Channel-Auto-Join

![](../../../../../.gitbook/assets/image%20%28371%29.png)

## Auto-join

{% hint style="info" %}
You can mark a channel **Auto-join** for members to join them automatically, once they join a team.
{% endhint %}

![](../../../../../.gitbook/assets/image%20%28352%29.png)
